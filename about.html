Origin Story

2109 N Pacifica Street San Jose Ca 12043

It all began in a small town in California and Mr. Shang dreamnt of becoming a resturant owner that carried delicious meals from his hometown in Thailand. He went to culinary school in San Fransisco in 1989 and opened his resturant six years later. His food brought delicious flavors and delicacies and he always emphasize bringing the culture of Thailand into his buisness so people know the food is authentic and unique compared to other buisnesses. A decade later his buisness became to blossom making Mr. Shang able to open up other locations as he wants to spread his love and passion for his culinary arts in different regions of Nothern California.
